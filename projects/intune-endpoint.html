Intune Endpoint Management Lab

Device compliance, Company Portal, Win32 app deployment, Intune Management Extension, Windows Update Rings, Windows LAPS and BitLocker.

Project overview

Built an Intune lab around CL01-ENTRA to practise modern endpoint administration and device-side troubleshooting.

Portfolio value: This project shows practical evidence, not just theory. The screenshots below show the portal results, command results and validation checks.
